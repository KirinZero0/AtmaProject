# Bowman

### Mob Document | Rangda's Infantry

---

## Overview

Ranged infantry — posted at distance to cover the melee soldiers and create pressure from angles Natha cannot immediately close. Not designed to be the primary threat. Designed to make every other threat harder to deal with.

---

## Appearance

Lighter armor than footsoldiers — mobility matters for repositioning. Carries a bow and a short blade as backup. Hood or helmet with better field of vision than standard infantry. Positioned high when terrain allows — walls, elevated ground, rooftops.

---

## Regions

Shore, Middle Ground, Cliff Temple, Capital Outskirts, Narendra.

---

## Combat

Keeps distance. Repositions when Natha closes in. Switches to the short blade in melee range — not comfortable there, uses it to create space to re-establish range.

**Attacks:**
- **Arrow** — standard ranged shot. Telegraphed by a brief draw animation. Dodgeable. Continuous pressure from range.
- **Charged Arrow** — longer draw, faster projectile, more damage. Visible by the extended hold before release.
- **Scatter** — fires multiple arrows in a spread at close-medium range. Used when Natha is approaching but not yet in melee.
- **Short Blade** — fast two-hit melee combo when Natha is in close range. No finesse — just buying distance. Parry hitbox active on first hit.

**Parry Hitbox:** Short Blade first hit only. No parry hitbox on any ranged attack.

---

## Notes

Bowmen are low priority in isolation. In groups with melee infantry they become high priority — the ranged pressure forces Natha into awkward positions during melee engagements.

Aerial attacks bypass most of their arrow arcs. Players who use the launch-and-aerial system effectively will deal with Bowmen from above.
