# Mutated Villager

### Mob Document | Consumed Civilian

---

## Overview

Civilians who consumed spirit beast meat — willingly or through the contaminated food supply — and have progressed far enough into consumption that the body no longer responds to the consciousness inside it. What remains is the corruption's aggression, operating a body that used to belong to someone.

On the Shore, these are villagers who followed Cakra — people who saw what their chief was becoming and, out of devotion or desperation, consumed spirit beast meat themselves. They are further along than the Middle Ground's consumed, who were dosed gradually through the food supply. These ones chose.

---

## Appearance

The progression is visible: bulging eyes, pale wrong skin, bone protrusions at the shoulders and jaw. Not as far as Cakra — he is the extreme end of what this looks like. These are the intermediate stages. Still recognizably human in shape. Dressed in Shore villager clothing, now torn and wrong-fitting as the body underneath changed.

They carry farming tools — whatever was in their hands or nearby when the consumption took over. Fishing hooks, net poles, hatchets, clay pots. Nothing designed for combat. All of it lethal anyway.

---

## Regions

Shore (Cakra's followers), Middle Ground (food supply victims), Capital Sewers (exposure victims — Sewer Mutant variant, further deteriorated).

---

## Combat

No technique. No tactics. Pure aggression — the corruption driving the body forward without strategy or self-preservation. They do not read Natha's movements. They do not react to being hit beyond momentary stagger. They simply advance and attack until they stop being able to.

**Attacks:**
- **Tool Swing** — wild horizontal or overhead swing with whatever they're carrying. Telegraphed by full-body windup. No finesse.
- **Lunge** — forward tackle. Closes distance fast. If it connects, brief grab state — Natha has to break free.
- **Frenzy** — when below half health, attack speed increases. No new moves, just faster. The corruption escalates when the body is losing.

**Parry Hitbox:** None. They have no technique — nothing in their movements is precise enough to generate a parry window.

---

## Notes

In groups they are dangerous through numbers and unpredictability — no coordinated pattern to read, just simultaneous aggression from multiple directions.

The Sewer Mutant variant (Capital Sewers) is further deteriorated — slower movement, but more damage per hit and higher health. The body has had longer to be rebuilt by the consumption.

Killing them is not liberation in the same sense as the Dedari or Garuda — they are human, not spirit world beings stranded in material form. What destroying the consumed body means for what remains of the person inside is a question the game does not answer comfortably.
