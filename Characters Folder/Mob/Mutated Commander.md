# Mutated Commander

### Mob Document | Rangda's Infantry — Rakshasa Enhanced

---

## Overview

Commanders who went through the Rakshasa cellular enhancement program. They retain their rank, their role, and their combat instincts — the enhancement did not erase what they were trained to be. It only changed the body delivering those instincts. The result is a commander's tactical mind in a frame that no longer fits through standard doorways.

---

## Appearance

Enormous — Rakshasa-proportioned, the original human commander buried under the bulk the enhancement forced on the frame. The rank markings are still there: the sash, the pauldron insignia, the command weapon — all of it now sitting on a body it was never sized for. The armor is split at the seams. The face is still recognizable if looked at long enough. Most don't look long enough.

---

## Regions

Capital Outskirts, Narendra.

---

## Combat

Slower than a standard Commander but significantly harder hitting. The Rally function remains — nearby soldiers still respond to it. The body's mass adds a new element: attacks that generate shockwaves or ground impact, and a presence that physically constrains the space around him.

**Attacks:**
- **Overhead Slam** — both fists down. Massive damage, ground shockwave in a short radius. Parry hitbox active on the downswing windup.
- **Backhand** — wide horizontal sweep with one arm. Fast for the size. Parry hitbox active.
- **Rally** — same as standard Commander. Raises weapon (or fist). Nearby soldiers buffed. Brief stationary window.
- **Charge** — short-range forward lunge. Closes distance faster than expected for the size. No parry hitbox — this is a grab/impact move.

**Parry Hitbox:** Overhead Slam windup, Backhand. NOT on Charge.

---

## Notes

The Mutated Commander hits hard enough that absorbing attacks without deflecting is not a reliable strategy. Players who fought standard Commanders expecting the same pattern will be corrected quickly.

Killing him still removes the Rally buff from nearby soldiers — the hierarchy still functions even in the enhanced form.
