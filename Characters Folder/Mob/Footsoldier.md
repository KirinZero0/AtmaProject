# Footsoldier

### Mob Document | Rangda's Infantry

---

## Overview

The backbone of Rangda's occupation force. Not elite, not specialized — just trained, disciplined, and present in large numbers. They hold posts, run patrols, enforce the lockdown orders, and stand between Natha and wherever he needs to go. Most of what Natha fights is this.

---

## Appearance

Standard-issue infantry armor — functional, maintained, uniform. Nothing ceremonial. Spear or short sword depending on assignment. Face covered by a visor or cloth wrap — individual identity is not the point of a footsoldier. They read as a unit, not as people.

---

## Regions

Shore, Middle Ground, Cliff Temple, Capital Outskirts, Narendra.

---

## Combat

Straightforward. Two to three melee attacks in sequence, a short recovery, repeat. They are not trying to be clever — they are trying to hold a line and outlast. In groups they space themselves to prevent Natha from isolating one without the others repositioning.

**Attacks:**
- **Thrust** — forward lunge with spear or sword. Telegraphed windup. Primary attack.
- **Sweep** — low horizontal swing. Used when Natha is too close for a thrust.
- **Shove** — non-damaging push. Creates distance when Natha is in melee range.

**Parry Hitbox:** Active on Thrust windup frames. A player attack during this window triggers deflect.

---

## Notes

Footsoldiers in groups defer to a Commander if one is present — they hold position while the Commander dictates pace. Without a Commander they are more aggressive but less coordinated.

They do not flee.
