# Garuda Unit

### Mob Document | Rangda's Infantry — Garuda Enhanced

---

## Overview

Soldiers enhanced with Garuda cellular material — the first successful product of the Cliff Temple experiments, and the proof of concept that made everything that followed possible. The Cliff Temple's location was not incidental: the disciples needed Garuda material, and Garuda were there. The experiments ran until the enhancement was reliable enough to deploy.

The result was soldiers that could fly. The logistical value alone justified the program. Everything else — the Tiger Guard, the Mutated Commanders, the Twin Gatekeepers — came after this worked.

---

## Appearance

Recognizably soldiers — the base frame is still human, still in uniform, still carrying military equipment. The wings are the deviation: large, feathered, unmistakably Garuda in origin, growing from the back and shoulders in a way that the original uniform was never designed to accommodate. The armor has been modified around them — cut away at the back, reinforced at the shoulders where the wings attach.

They carry bows. Flight and range together. The combination was the point.

---

## Regions

Cliff Temple primarily. Appear elsewhere in later areas as aerial support units alongside ground infantry.

---

## Combat

Stays airborne when possible. Uses height to establish firing angles that ground-based attacks cannot easily reach. Descends to reposition or when forced down. Not designed for melee — the bow is the weapon, flight is the platform.

**Attacks:**
- **Arrow** — standard ranged shot from altitude. Fires while hovering or in slow drift. Dodgeable but the angle changes depending on position relative to Natha.
- **Dive Shot** — descends rapidly and fires at close range at the bottom of the dive before pulling back up. Fast. Harder to dodge than standard Arrow due to proximity. Parry hitbox: none — ranged attack.
- **Dive Strike** — unarmed descent, uses wing and momentum as a strike. Melee range. Used when bow is ineffective at close distance. Parry hitbox active on impact frame.
- **Ascend** — breaks away from melee range by flying upward. Repositions to ranged distance. Not an attack.

**Parry Hitbox:** Dive Strike impact only. No parry hitbox on any ranged attack.

---

## Notes

Garuda Units are the inverse of Bowmen — where Bowmen are ground units that lose effectiveness at range, Garuda Units are aerial units that lose effectiveness up close. Natha's aerial combat system — launching enemies, attacking in the air — is the most direct counter. Getting a Garuda Unit into the air on Natha's terms is better than waiting for them to dictate the engagement from altitude.

The wings are Garuda material but the soldiers are still human. What the Cliff Temple records document, in clinical language, is how many subjects the enhancement killed before the wing attachment became survivable. The Garuda Units flying over the temple are the ones it didn't kill.
