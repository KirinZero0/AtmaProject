# Spirit Beast Dog

### Mob Document | Militarized Spirit Beast — Shore

---

## Overview

Spirit world beings that took canine form when the corrupted land built them material bodies — the available template was dog, and the land built accordingly. Left to themselves they would be aggressive but directionless, like all stranded spirit beasts operating on the corruption's instinct rather than their own nature.

Rangda's disciples found them and made them directionless in a specific direction. Militarized — trained to respond to soldier commands, stationed at patrol routes, used as tracking and assault units alongside the Shore's infantry.

---

## Appearance

Dog-shaped but wrong. The corruption's version of a dog: too large, the proportions slightly off, the fur replaced by something that sits closer to the spirit world's texture — translucent at the edges, the body not fully committing to material solidity. The eyes are wrong. The eyes are always wrong on spirit world beings given material form by corrupted land.

No equipment. No collar or marking — the disciples did not need to brand them. They follow commands because the commands were trained into them while the corruption was still fresh.

---

## Regions

Shore.

---

## Combat

Fast. Low to the ground. Closes distance quickly and attacks from angles soldiers cannot cover. Works alongside infantry — the soldiers hold Natha's attention while the dogs flank. Against Natha alone, they circle and feint before committing.

**Attacks:**
- **Bite** — fast lunge-and-snap. Short range, very fast. Primary attack.
- **Tackle** — full-body lunge. Knocks Natha back on hit. Longer cooldown than Bite.
- **Flank** — repositions quickly to Natha's side or behind. Not an attack — a movement that sets up the next Bite from a worse angle.
- **Howl** — signals nearby soldiers. Not damaging. Used when Natha is detected in stealth sections or when the dog is isolated and losing. Draws reinforcements if any are in range.

**Parry Hitbox:** None. Flying unit rules apply — fast, low, non-technique movement doesn't generate parry windows.

---

## Notes

Spirit Beast Dogs are the liberation-applies case: destroying the material body frees the spirit being inside from the corruption's control. The aggression is the corruption's, not theirs. This is not communicated in combat. It is available in the lore for players who look.

In groups with soldiers they are the primary threat — soldiers become cover for the dogs rather than the reverse.
