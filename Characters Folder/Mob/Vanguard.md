# Vanguard

### Mob Document | Rangda's Infantry

---

## Overview

Heavy frontline infantry — the soldiers positioned first into a fight to absorb and hold. Built around endurance rather than speed. Where footsoldiers form a line, Vanguards are the line. They are not fast. They do not need to be.

---

## Appearance

The heaviest armor in the standard infantry roster — layered plate, a large shield on one arm, a heavy weapon on the other. Moves with the deliberate weight of someone who has learned not to hurry. The shield bears Rangda's insignia — functional iconography, the symbol of what they serve displayed on the thing they use to stop you.

---

## Regions

Middle Ground, Cliff Temple, Capital Outskirts, Narendra.

---

## Combat

Advances slowly and does not retreat. The shield absorbs frontal attacks — Natha cannot break through with direct hits and needs to work around the side or force the Vanguard to drop it. Patient and deliberate. Does not overcommit.

**Attacks:**
- **Shield Bash** — forward shove with the shield. Staggers Natha on hit. Creates space. Parry hitbox active on the bash motion.
- **Heavy Swing** — wide slow arc with the main weapon. High damage, long reach. Parry hitbox active during windup.
- **Advance** — slow deliberate step forward, shield raised. Not a damaging attack — a repositioning move that pushes Natha back if he doesn't move. Forces engagement on Vanguard's terms.

**Parry Hitbox:** Shield Bash, Heavy Swing windup.

**Shield:** Frontal attacks are blocked — no damage, no deflect trigger. Natha must flank or bait the Vanguard into lowering the shield (after a Heavy Swing recovery, the shield drops briefly).

---

## Notes

Vanguards in groups are stationary anchor points — footsoldiers and other infantry maneuver around them. Breaking the Vanguard opens the formation.

The brief window after a Heavy Swing — shield lowered, weapon recovering — is the primary opening. Elite players will aerial attack over the shield.
