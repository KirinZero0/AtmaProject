# Elite Guard

### Mob Document | Rangda's Infantry

---

## Overview

The upper tier of Rangda's military — soldiers selected for competence and stationed at high-priority locations. Not enhanced, not experimental. Just better trained, better equipped, and more dangerous than the footsoldiers below them. They appear where Rangda needs something held by force of skill rather than numbers.

---

## Appearance

Heavier armor, better maintained. Carries a weapon suited to their specialty — heavy sword, reinforced spear, or a paired blade configuration. Some wear partial face masks — not the ceremonial Topeng of the true elite, just functional visor work. The difference between an Elite Guard's mask and the Gatekeeper's mask is immediately legible.

---

## Regions

Cliff Temple, Capital Outskirts, Narendra.

---

## Combat

Reads Natha more carefully than a footsoldier. Adapts after taking hits — changes attack timing, repositions, switches between offensive and defensive posture. Has a larger moveset and uses it.

**Attacks:**
- **Heavy Strike** — slow, powerful blow with significant range. Parry hitbox active on windup.
- **Combination** — two-hit sequence, second hit faster than the first. Parry hitbox on first hit only.
- **Guard** — raises weapon into a defensive stance. Blocks Natha's attacks for a short duration. Does not parry — absorbs hits. Ends with a riposte if Natha continues attacking through it.
- **Riposte** — fast counter after Guard absorption. Parry hitbox active.

**Parry Hitbox:** Heavy Strike windup, first hit of Combination, Riposte.

---

## Notes

Elite Guards in groups cover each other's guard windows — one holds defensive stance while another attacks. Natha has to break the pattern rather than just dealing damage.

They are the closest thing to the Gatekeeper bosses in terms of combat identity. Fighting an Elite Guard is a preview of what the Gatekeepers do, compressed.
