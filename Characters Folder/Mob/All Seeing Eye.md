# All Seeing Eye

### Mob Document | Rangda's Construct

---

## Overview

A spirit world construct — Rangda's eye in the field. Not a being with its own nature or will. A tool: conjured, deployed, maintained by Rangda's magic for as long as she needs it there. It drifts alongside patrols, watching. Sometimes it wanders on its own through areas soldiers do not cover. It sees everything in its range and everything it sees, Rangda sees.

It can be killed in one hit. Rangda can make more.

---

## Appearance

A large eye — spirit world geometry, the iris wrong in color and movement, fire drifting around it like a corona. No body. No limbs. Just the eye and the fire and the slow, deliberate rotation as it scans. It hovers at head height or above, adjusting to terrain. In dark areas the fire is the first thing visible. In daylight it is the wrongness of it — the eye that belongs to no face, watching.

---

## Regions

All regions where Rangda's soldiers are present. Appears alongside patrols as a supplementary surveillance unit and occasionally drifts through areas independently.

---

## Behavior

Follows patrol routes when attached to a soldier group — drifts slightly ahead or to the side, scanning angles the soldiers themselves cannot cover. When wandering independently it moves slowly, no fixed route, covering open ground and approaches.

If it detects Natha it does not immediately attack — it alerts. Soldiers in range respond to the alert. The Eye continues to track Natha's position while soldiers engage, maintaining visibility so the soldiers always know where he is.

If Natha is not engaged with soldiers — if the Eye is alone with him — it attacks directly.

---

## Combat

**Attacks:**
- **Fire Bolt** — launches a projectile of spirit world fire. Slow moving, moderate damage. Fired at range.
- **Fire Burst** — close range explosion of the corona fire. Used when Natha is directly adjacent to it. Immediate, no telegraph.

**Parry Hitbox:** None. Construct, no technique, no physical strike.

**Health:** One hit. Any attack destroys it.

---

## Notes

Priority target in any encounter where one is present. As long as the Eye is active, soldiers know Natha's exact position — flanking, repositioning, breaking line of sight all become less effective. Destroying it first costs one attack and removes a significant tactical disadvantage.

The fire does not go out when it dies. It disperses — the corona scatters, embers drift, then nothing. Whatever Rangda put into it returns to wherever she pulled it from.

She will send another one eventually.
