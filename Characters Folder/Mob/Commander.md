# Commander

### Mob Document | Rangda's Infantry

---

## Overview

Field commanders — the soldiers who lead the units, read the situation, and keep the others in formation. More dangerous alone, significantly more dangerous in a group. Priority target: killing the Commander disrupts the unit's coordination and removes their combat buff.

---

## Appearance

Heavier armor than footsoldiers, with rank markings — a different pauldron, a sash, a helmet crest. Carries a longer sword or a ceremonial weapon that still functions as a weapon. Visibly distinct from the infantry around them even at a distance. This is intentional — they are meant to be identified as authority.

---

## Regions

Shore, Middle Ground, Cliff Temple, Capital Outskirts, Narendra.

---

## Combat

Fights more deliberately than a footsoldier — reads Natha's position before committing, covers the soldiers around him. His personal attacks are slower but harder and carry more range.

**Attacks:**
- **Command Strike** — a single powerful downward blow. Long windup, high damage. Parry hitbox active during windup.
- **Counter Slash** — reacts to Natha's attacks with a fast sideways cut. Triggered only when Natha is in melee range and attacking.
- **Rally** — non-attack action. Raises weapon briefly — nearby footsoldiers gain increased speed and attack frequency for a short duration. Commander is briefly stationary during Rally.

**Parry Hitbox:** Active on Command Strike windup and Counter Slash.

---

## Notes

The Rally buff is visible — affected footsoldiers move faster and hit harder until the duration ends or the Commander dies. Killing the Commander mid-Rally ends the buff immediately.

Commanders do not Rally if they are the only enemy remaining. They focus entirely on Natha.
