# The Calonarang Cycle

## The Original Incident

The Calonarang was never fully resolved. [[Rangda]] was sealed, not destroyed. The ritual that bound her was performed by a king wearing the Barong mask — channeling Barong's protective power to contain her chaos. It worked. The kingdom survived.

But sealing is not ending. She sleeps, she gathers, and she returns.

## The 800-Year Cycle

For eight hundred years the cycle held. [[Rangda]] would awaken. A king of the bloodline would don the Barong mask, perform the ritual, and seal her again. The knowledge was preserved, the lineage maintained, the cost paid generation after generation. It was a wound that never healed — but it was managed.

Nobody questioned whether it could last forever. It had always worked.

## The Breaking

King Dharma refused.

The reasons are his own — whether fear, pride, doubt, or something else is not recorded cleanly. What is recorded is the outcome: he did not don the mask. He faced [[Rangda]] without it. He lost.

Without the ritual, without the vessel, [[Rangda]] was not sealed. She was free.

Devata Island fell under her dominion. The kingdom collapsed. What remained of the royal bloodline scattered. the conspiracy went underground. The mask went silent, waiting.

## 200 Years Later

[[Rangda]] has ruled Devata for two centuries. The island has been rotting under her dominion long enough that most of its people have never known anything else.

Natha — a descendant of Dharma, 200 years removed, with no knowledge of what he carries in his blood — comes to Devata looking for his missing father. He does not come to break the cycle. He does not know there is a cycle to break.

the conspiracy finds him first. They put the mask on his face before he can say no.

The mask recognizes the bloodline immediately. It has been waiting two hundred years for this.
