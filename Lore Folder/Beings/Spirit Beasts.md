# Spirit Beasts

### Beings Lore | Spirit World

---

## What They Are

Spirit beasts are not a single species. The term refers to any spirit world being that has become stranded in the material world long enough for the corrupted land to build them a material body.

The spirit world and the material world are meant to be separated by the veil — the boundary that keeps each in its proper place. When the veil breaks or thins, spirit world beings cross over. Most do not intend to stay. The corrupted land does not ask their intentions. It builds them a body from what is available, shaped according to what they are, and the being finds itself embodied in a world it was not designed for.

The material body is a corruption — not of the being's nature, but of their situation. What emerges is not what they are. It is what the corrupted land made from them.

---

## The Aggression

Spirit beasts in the game are aggressive. This is not their nature.

The aggression belongs to the corruption — to the material body the broken veil and the corrupted land produced. The being inside the body is still what it always was. It simply cannot reach the surface. The body acts on the corruption's instincts: territorial, hostile, driven by impulses the spirit world being would not recognize as theirs.

This matters for how they are encountered and how they are ended.

---

## Liberation

Destroying the material body is not killing the spirit world being inside it. The body is the corruption's construction. When it is destroyed, the being is released — returned to the spirit world, freed from the material form that was never theirs to begin with.

The aggression ends with the body. Whatever the spirit beast actually was, underneath the corruption's work, goes back to where it came from.

This applies to all spirit beasts. It does not apply to consumed humans — they are not spirit world beings stranded in corrupted material form. The distinction matters.

---

## Militarization

Rangda's disciples identified that spirit beasts, while uncontrolled, were a wasted resource. The corruption's aggression was already present — already producing beings that attacked without instruction. The disciples introduced instruction.

Militarized spirit beasts respond to soldier commands, hold assigned positions, and function as weapons rather than unmanaged hazards. The being inside is still in there, unable to reach the surface, now also unable to direct the body away from orders it did not choose to follow.

The Spirit Beast Dogs of the Shore are an example. Whatever they were in the spirit world — whatever their nature actually was — they are now patrol units on a coastal military posting, responding to infantry commands.

## Notes

Spirit beasts are rare encounters. They are not common soldiers or routine enemies. When one appears it is an event — something that was a spirit world being, built a wrong body by a broken world, now unable to be what it was. The rarity is intentional. It preserves the weight of what each one represents.

Environmental critters — small animals, birds, creatures that inhabit the world without engaging Natha — are not spirit beasts. They are simply animals. The distinction is visible: spirit beasts are wrong in the specific way the corrupted land's constructions are wrong. Animals are just animals.
